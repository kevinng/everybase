What does Everybase do?

Many companies forward supply chain documents in a predictable and process-driven manner.

For example, a chemical distributor receives COA and SDS from its suppliers and sends them to its customer on delivery.

Everybase lets you associate material/batch codes to documents.

Its API lets your ERP trigger the right documents to send to your customers.

And your customers access their documents via a self-service portal.

API-Driven

Everybase fits nicely into your processes. You can perform most actions via the API.

Web-Based Console

We provide a web-based console to make it easier for your staff to upload documents and for your customers to access documents they've received.

Software-as-a-Service

No hardware to manage. Fewer IT personnel needed. Quicker deployment. Easy to scale.

Fanatical Support

We're here for you every step of the way - from integrating Everybase to your ERP to ensuring your customers get their documents.

Managing and Sending Documents

No Wrangling with Filenames

Many companies use shared drives to manage documents. Filenames often include details such as material code - so co-workers can look them up. Everybase lets you link details such as material code with files directly.

Send Documents With or Without Humans

You may send documents from Everybase without writing emails and attaching files. Alternatively, your systems may call Everybase's API to send it for you.

Receiving Documents

Focused Email Message

Document recipients receive emails with document links. You decide the documents that go into a message. For example, a message may be associated with a set of DO/SO/PO references, with the list of documents for the products in the delivery order under it.

We Handle the Tech

Email display is responsive and optimized for most clients. We work with you to handle deliverability issues. No large emails that are bloated with attachments. Secured links ensure only intended recipients have access to the documents.

Accessing Documents

Personal Inbox

Each user has a personal inbox with the documents they've received, so you won't have to resend documents.

Case Study - Chemical Distributor

A chemical distributor accompanies deliveries with documents such as COA, SDS, TDS. Everybase lets you send these documents automatically when you issue a delivery order.

Example Use Case - Chemical Distributor
  1. 1

    You: Issue DO via Your ERP System

    Your ERP system calls Everybase's secured web API when a delivery order is successfully issued. In this call is a list of material and batch codes for the products specified in the delivery order.

  2. 2

    Everybase: Send Secured Document Links via Email

    Everybase sends your customer an email with links to the documents plus essential references, including your DO/SO codes and the customer's PO code.

  3. 3

    Your Customer: Access Documents via Self-Service Portal

    Only intended recipients may access the documents through the secured links.
